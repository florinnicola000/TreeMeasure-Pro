Features

Everything you need in the field

Professional forestry tools, right on your LiDAR-equipped iPhone.

📏

LiDAR Diameter

The depth sensor scans the trunk and calculates diameter at breast height (DBH) with ±1–2 cm accuracy — no caliper required.

📐

AR Height

Real-time augmented clinometer: point at the tree top and base, and the app calculates height using trigonometry and motion sensors.

🌳

Fixed Plot

Standard inventory with configurable plot radius. Record each tree and the app automatically calculates basal area and total volume.

🔭

Bitterlich Method

Integrated digital relascope. Hold the phone at arm's length, count the included trees — instant result in m²/ha.

🗺️

GPS Map

Every record is geolocated and displayed on a map. Navigate between plots and track your inventory progress easily.

🧮

Volume & Coefficients

Built-in form factor table for wood volume calculation. Select the species, the app does the rest.

📊

CSV Export

Export all records as CSV, compatible with Excel, Numbers, and any forestry software. Ready for the office.

📡

100% Offline

All features work without an internet connection. Perfect for remote areas without mobile coverage.

🌿

Species Database

Database of native forest species with autocomplete and scientific names included for quick identification.

How it works

Simple from the very first tree

From opening the app to exporting your data — in just a few easy steps.

1

Choose a method

Select your inventory method: LiDAR diameter, AR height, fixed plot, or Bitterlich.

2

Point the camera

Aim at the tree. AR guides help you position the phone correctly for optimal results.

3

Measure

Press the measure button. LiDAR and the motion sensor compute the values automatically.

4

Save & export

The record is saved with GPS and a timestamp. When done, export the full inventory as CSV.

Forestry methods

Four professional methods

Silviculturally validated techniques, digitized for field and office use.

📏

LiDAR Diameter

Replaces the traditional caliper. The 3D sensor reconstructs the trunk profile and extracts DBH.

📐

AR Clinometer

Replaces the Haga hypsometer. Height measurement via augmented reality and high-precision gyroscope.

🌳

Fixed Plot

Complete inventory on a defined area. Automatic stand calculations: N/ha, basal area, volume.

🔭

Relascope Sampling

Bitterlich method with configurable BAF angles. Direct result in basal area per hectare.
